《Bi-LSTM Chinese Word Sense Disambiguation Model Fused Linguistic Knowledge》—— Author:XiPing Hao\ShuDong Liu
  In this paper, we propose the model of Bi-LSTM Chinese WSD fused the linguistic knowledge of word, which include the ambiguity word sense label, context features, dictionary knowledge, and the hypernymy-hyponymy relations etc. In the framework of the Bi-LSTM Chinese WSD model, the context module and the linguistic knowledge fusion module are jointly encoded to improve the accuracy of WSD.
《Word-based Disambiguation Based on Neural Network》—— Author:XiPing Hao\ShuDong Liu
  This paper proposes a neural network-based model to achieve word sense disambiguation, input word vector into neural network, and achieve the purpose of disambiguation through classification. Experiments show that neural network disambiguation has higher accuracy than traditional statistical method disambiguation. 【Key words】: Word sense disambiguation; Natural language processing; Word vector; Neural network
《An intelligent online learning platform based on post ability demand》—— Author:XiPing Hao\ShuDong Liu
  我们以“学生就业需求-招聘信息对接-技能实体提取-对应课程推荐”为开发链式进行系统设计。通过提供对外检索接口让用户输入自己感兴趣的岗位或者技能名称，利用分词技术对搜索内容进行拆分，后台通过倒排索引的方式提供招聘信息文档集，并根据单词权重进行评分，将得分较高的招聘信息优先显示。利用Bi-LSTM-CRF实体识别技术自动的对技能实体进行提取，并针对提取的技能实体进行相应的课程推荐，这些课程信息与招聘信息利用分布式爬虫技术从模拟浏览器中爬取。
